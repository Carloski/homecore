DOCUMENTATION OF THE DEVELOPMENT PROCESS

Intent & Purpose

The main purpose of the website is to serve as a Point of contact and lead capturing instrument for the company. Aside from showcasing the services offered, it includes call to action elements such as a contact form in the showcase area and a free newsletter sign up at the bottom of the first page.

Design Choices

When I was thinking about building this site, I knew I wanted to make it clean, somewhat modern and something I would like to visit. I didn't want anything too busy that could be hard to navigate. If the information is hard to get to or takes too many clicks to get information, users tend to leave and not return.

I also added some CSS effects in the about page as well as in the navigation and social lins to make it more attractive.

Opportunities for Improvement and Growth

I would've liked to make it more responsive. Right now is very simple because I had a hard time making some of the elements look the way I wanted when viewed in smaller screens.

Here are some elements I would add in the future

  • Blog post
  • Downloadable content
  • A log in option for clients and contractors.
