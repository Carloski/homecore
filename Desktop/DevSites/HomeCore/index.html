HOMECORE

is a statewide property services company founded in 1978. We provide property inspections, property preservation, property maintenance, rehab & repair, property registrations, HOA and utility services and other property-related services in all 67 counties in the state of Florida.

The success of Homecore is the result of our continuous focus on providing quality services and building long-term relationships with mortgage servicers based upon trust and integrity. We strive to hire the best people in our industry who share our values and are committed to providing exceptional service. Homecore has over 40 years experience in the preservation and maintenenace industry, rivaling that of any other field services company.

We are fortunate to serve some of the industry's most respected mortgage and tenenant servicing companies. We take responsibility on behalf of our clients by protecting and preserving communities all across the Great state of Florida and helping stabilize neighborhoods by focusing on:

  • Quality
  • Technology
  • Compliance
  • Experience
